Mission

Why Spacepoint exists and what success looks like.

Driving awareness and participation in space exploration. We measure success by participation—people showing up, learning, building, and taking the next step toward the space ecosystem.

Over time, this becomes a pipeline: inspiration → education → hands-on experience → opportunity.
